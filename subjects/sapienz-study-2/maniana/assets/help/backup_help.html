Maniana Backup and Restore

Backing up Maniana tasks requires the Dropbox (recommended) or Google Drive free apps on this device.

Note: Recent restrictions in the GMail app made it incompatible with Maniana backup.

To Backup:
Click on Do Backup and Select Dropbox or Google Drive to upload the file.

To Restore:
Open the file in the Dropbox or Google Drive apps and import into Maniana.

Press the Back button to continue...
